I am a Research Fellow at Microsoft Research India. I am a part of the reasoning team, under Akshay Nambi, where we work on cutting-edge research in reasoning models and AI agents. Before this I worked with Prof. Tanmoy Chakraborty, who was my thesis advisor, and the work done by us was published at TACL'24. Earlier, I had the opportunity to work with Prof. Chetan Arora, with whom I published my very first paper in CVPR'24 which was on monocular depth estimation. Before this, I worked with Prof. Rohan Paul on robust verbal communication with field robots through the use of contextual correction of transcriptions.
